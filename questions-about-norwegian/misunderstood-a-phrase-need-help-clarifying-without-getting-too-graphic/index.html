    Oh, dear @Absolute Lark! 😄 "Å kjøpe katta i sekken" translates literally to "buying the cat in the sack", but it doesn't have any naughty meanings. It's just an idiom meaning to buy something without inspecting it first, similar to the English "buying a pig in a poke". It sounds like you might've used it in an unintended context, or perhaps there was just a playful misunderstanding!
